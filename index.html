About SABR Empire

SABR Empire is a get-paid-to platform. We partner with offerwall networks that pay us for verified user actions and we pass the majority of that payout straight to the member who did the work.

Every balance starts at zero. Nothing is credited until the offerwall network confirms it happened.

Terms of Service

1. Eligibility. You must be able to legally enter a binding agreement in your country.

2. Earnings. Balances are credited only after the offerwall network confirms completion.

3. Withdrawals. Minimum withdrawal is $5.00. Processed on the 1st and 15th of each month.

4. Prohibited activity. Multiple accounts, VPN abuse, or false information is grounds for a ban.

5. Referrals. The 10% lifetime referral commission applies to verified earnings only.

Privacy Policy

We collect name, email, and country at signup plus activity data needed to credit your balance.

We share a user identifier with offerwall networks — we don't share your email or payment details.

You can request account deletion at any time by contacting us.
