About SABR Empire

SABR Empire is a get-paid-to platform. We partner with offerwall networks that pay us for verified user actions — completing a survey, trying an app, finishing a trial — and we pass the majority of that payout straight to the member who did the work. We keep a share to cover network fees, payment processing and hosting.

Every balance on this site starts at zero. Nothing is credited until the offerwall network confirms it happened. We don't display fabricated member counts, earnings, or leaderboard entries — what you see reflects real activity only.

Terms of Service

1. Eligibility. You must be able to legally enter a binding agreement in your country of residence and provide accurate signup information.

2. Earnings. Balances are credited only after the relevant offerwall network confirms completion. Networks may reverse a conversion (chargeback) up to their stated holding period; reversed conversions will be deducted from your balance.

3. Withdrawals. Minimum withdrawal is $5.00. Requests are processed in batches on the 1st and 15th of each month. We may request identity verification before releasing a withdrawal.

4. Prohibited activity. Creating multiple accounts, using VPNs/proxies to bypass offer geo-restrictions, or submitting false information to a network is grounds for an account ban and forfeiture of unpaid balance.

5. Referrals. The 10% lifetime referral commission applies to verified earnings only and may be modified for new referrals with notice.

6. Changes. We may update these terms; continued use after a change means you accept the update.

Privacy Policy

We collect the information you give us at signup (name, email, country) plus the activity data needed to credit your balance (offer completions, withdrawal requests, referral relationships).

We share a user identifier with offerwall networks so they can attribute completed offers back to your account — we don't share your email or payment details with them.

Payment details you submit for a withdrawal (wallet address, UPI ID, etc.) are used only to process that withdrawal.

You can request account deletion at any time by contacting us; unpaid balances below the minimum withdrawal are forfeited on deletion.
